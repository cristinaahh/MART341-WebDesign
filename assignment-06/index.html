Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of green beans
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • About 2/3 cup of dried onions

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Place green beans and cream of mushroom soup in a pan then stir together
  3. Cook for about 25 minutes after the oven is preheated
  4. Take the pan out and add the onions to the top and cook till the onions are golden brown, about 5 minutes
